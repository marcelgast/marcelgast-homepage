  <div class="section-inner">
    <h2 class="section-title about-title">Music, Software, and Visual Direction</h2>
    <div class="about-showcase">
      <article class="about-panel about-panel--copy">
        <p class="lead">
          I’m Marcel, a guitarist, songwriter, and developer focused on building projects that feel as good to use as they do to hear.
          <br><br>
          Music is at the center of everything I do. Xerador is my main groove metal band and the place where most of my creative energy goes into riffs, arrangements, and songwriting. Katalyst is my solo outlet where every decision stays fully in my hands, from composition to production. Frostfire Wings is a collaborative project built with a close friend and vocalist, where we push into a different sound and experiment beyond the boundaries of my other work.
          <br><br>
          Software is the second pillar. I build practical tools that solve real musician problems. PracticeTab came from daily practice frustration and is built to reduce distraction, remove workflow friction, and make sessions more consistent. TempoDeck extends that idea to rehearsal and live performance, with tempo mapping, cue systems, and setlist flow designed for bands that need precision on stage.
          <br><br>
          I also design and build custom websites for musicians, bands, and creative businesses. My focus is clear structure, strong brand presence, and modern front-end execution, so each site feels specific to the project instead of generic.
          <br><br>
          Across all three areas, my standard is the same: high quality, strong identity, and systems that are simple to use but deep enough to grow with.
